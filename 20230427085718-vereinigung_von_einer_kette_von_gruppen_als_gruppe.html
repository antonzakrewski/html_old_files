union of a chain of groups as group

1. Proposition

Let $G1 \subseteq G2 \subseteq G3 … $ be a chain of groups. Then \(G \coloneqq \bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} G_n\) is also a group

2. Proof

2.1. monoid

cf. union of a chain of monoids as monoid

2.2. inverse element

Let \(g_1 \in G\), then by assumption there exists a \(G_i\) such that \(g_1 \in G_i\) and hence \(g_1^{-1} \in G_i \subseteq G\)
