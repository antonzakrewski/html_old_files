evaluation map from a locally compact as continuous map

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T})\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, where \(Y\) is locally regular compact Then the evaluation

\begin{align*} \sigma: \mathrm{Map}(Y,Z) \times \mathrm{Map}(*,Y) \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}(*,Z) \end{align*}

is continuous
