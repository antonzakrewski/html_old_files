vanishing set of the generated ideal as closure in the zariski topology

1. Satz

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(\mathbb{A}_K^n\) the affine space and \(Y \subesteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) a subset. then for the topological closure in the zariski topology on the affine space, it holds that

\begin{align*} \overline{Y} = Z(I(Y)) \end{align*}

where \(Z\) is the zero locus and \(I\) is the vanishing ideal

2. Beweis

2.1. a)

\begin{align*} Y \subseteq V(I(V)) \end{align*}

und ferner ist

\begin{align*} V(I(Y)) \end{align*}

nach definition abgeschlossen

Damit folgt

\begin{align*} \overline{Y} \subseteq V(I(Y)) \end{align*}
