commutativity of follow by and preceed by morphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\alpha, \beta, \gamma\) suitable morphisms such that the composition \(\alpha \circ \beta \circ \gamma\) is defined. Then for the follow by morphism and preceed by morphism it holds:

\begin{align*} \gamma^{ \rightarrow} \circ \alpha^{ \leftarrow} = \alpha^{ \leftarrow } \circ \gamma^{ \rightarrow} \end{align*}

2. Proof

By construction we know:

\begin{align*} \alpha \circ \beta \circ \gamma =& (\alpha \circ \beta) \circ \gamma \\ =& \gamma^{ \rightarrow } (\alpha \circ \beta) \\ =& \gamma^{ \rightarrow } (\alpha^{ \leftarrow}(\beta)) \\ \alpha \circ \beta \circ \gamma =& \alpha \circ (\beta \circ \gamma) \\ =& \alpha^{ \leftarrow}(\beta \circ \gamma) \\ =& \alpha^{ \leftarrow}(\gamma^{ \rightarrow}(\gamma)) \\ \end{align*}

and therefore we conclude:

\begin{align*} \gamma^{ \rightarrow} \circ \alpha^{ \leftarrow} = \alpha^{ \leftarrow} \circ \gamma^{ \rightarrow} \end{align*}
