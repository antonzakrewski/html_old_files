induced map between spectra by a ring homomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be rings, \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) a ring-homomorphism and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A),\mathrm{Spec}(B)\) the zariski spectrum Then

\begin{align*} \varphi^*: \mathrm{Spec}(B) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \\ \mathfrak{p} \mapsto& \varphi^{-1}[\mathfrak{p}] \end{align*}

is a welldefined map

2. Proof

Preimage of a prime ideal as prime ideal
