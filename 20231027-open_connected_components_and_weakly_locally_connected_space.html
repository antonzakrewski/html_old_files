open connected components and weakly locally connected space

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space.

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is weakly locally connected
  2. each connected component \(C(x)\) is open

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Let \(C(x)\) be a connected component and \(y \in C(x)\). Then there exists a connected open neighbourhood \(O\), hence by Vereinigung von überlappenden zusammenhängenden Mengen, it follows, that

\begin{align*} O \cup C(x) \end{align*}

is also connected. Thus by maximality of \(C(x)\), it follows that \(O \subseteq C(x)\). Therefore, \(C(x)\) is a neighbourhood of \(y\) and since \(y\) was arbitrary, it follows, that \(C(x)\) is a neighbourhood of all its points. Hence \(C(x)\) is open (cf. openness and neighbourhood for all points)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

take \(C(y)\) as connected, open neighbourhood
