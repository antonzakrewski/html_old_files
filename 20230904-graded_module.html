graded module

1. Definition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module.

Then \(M\) is said to be a (compatibly) graded module, if \(M = \bigoplus M_n\) and for \(n,m \in \mathbb{N}_0\)

\begin{align*} R_n M_m \subseteq M_{n+m} \end{align*}
