noetherian space and maximal element for the finite union of open subset

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space

  1. \(X\) is a noetherian space
  2. Let \(S_i \subseteq X\) be family of subsets and \(\mathcal{S}\) the set of all finite intersections.

Then there exists a minimal element

2. Proof

Since the map \(\psi: A \rightarrow X \setminus A\) reverses order, this statement is dual to noetherian space and minimal element of finite intersections of closed subsets
