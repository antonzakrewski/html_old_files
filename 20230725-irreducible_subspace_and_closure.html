irreducible subspace and closure

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(T \subseteq X\) be a subset.

TFAE:

  1. \(T\) is irreducible.
  2. the closure \(\overline{T}\) is irreducible

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Suppose \(T\) is irreducible and there exist closed sets \(C_1,C_2\) such that

\begin{align*} \overline{T} = C_1 \cup C_2 \end{align*}

Assuming that \(T \cap C_1 = \emptyset\), we conclude that

\begin{align*} T \subseteq C_2 \end{align*}

and by the characterization of the closure as smallest closed set we deduce

\begin{align*} \overline{T} \supseteq C_2 \end{align*}

Therefore \(C_2 \cap \overline{T} = \overline{T}\) and by definition \(\overline{T}\) is irreducible

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
