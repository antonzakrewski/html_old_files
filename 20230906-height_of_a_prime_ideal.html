height of a prime ideal

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq A\) a prime ideal. Then the height \(\mathrm{ht}(\mathfrak{p})\) of \(\mathfrak{p}\) is defined as supremum

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ht}(\mathfrak{p}) = \mathrm{sup} \{n \vert J_0 \subsetneq J_1 ... \subsetneq J_n = J\} \end{align*}
