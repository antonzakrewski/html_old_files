product of homogeneous elements as homogeneous element

1. Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(a \in R_n, b \in R_m\) a homogeneous element Then \(a \cdot b\) is a homogeneous element

2. Proof

follows from \(R_n \cdot R_m \subseteq R_{n+m}\), hence \(a \cdot b \in R_{n+m}\)
