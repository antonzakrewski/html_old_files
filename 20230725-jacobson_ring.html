Jacobson ring

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring. Then \(A\) is a Jacobson ring, if for all prime ideals \(\mathfrak{p}\) it holds

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p} = \bigcap \{\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A \vert \mathfrak{m} \text{ maximal with }\mathfrak{p} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}\} \end{align*}
