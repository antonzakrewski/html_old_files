smooth map between manifolds

1. Definition

Let \(M,N\) be a smooth manifolds and \(f: M \rightarrow N\) a map. then \(f\) is said to be smooth, if for all \(p \in M\) there exists

  1. a chart \((U,\pi)\) of \(M\) with \(p \in U\)
  2. a chart \((V, \psi)\) of \(N\) with \(f(p) \in V\)

such that

\begin{align*} \psi \circ f \circ f: \mathbb{R}^m \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^n \end{align*}

is a glatte Funktion
