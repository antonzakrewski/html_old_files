length of a module

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Then the length \(l(M)\) of \(M\) is defined as

\begin{align*} l(M) \coloneqq \inf \{n \in \mathbb{N} \vert \exists \text{composition series with lenght } m\} \end{align*}

see: length of a chain of submodules, composition series
