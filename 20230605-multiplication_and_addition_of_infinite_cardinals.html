Multiplication and Addition of infinite Cardinals

1. Proposition

Let \(\lambda, \kappa\) be infinite (right link ?) Cardinals, then it follows for the Multiplication of cardinals and Addition of Cardinals

\begin{align*} \kappa \oplus \lambda =& \kappa \odot \lambda \\ =& \mathrm{max}(\kappa, \lambda) \end{align*}
