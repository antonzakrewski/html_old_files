commutative ring as jacobson ring and quotient of ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring. TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is a Jacobson ring
  2. for an ideal \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\) and the quotient ring we conclude \(\mathcal{N}_{A/\mathfrak{a}} = \mathcal{J}_{A/\mathfrak{a}}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

  • jacobson ideal as nilradical for a jacobson ring
  • quotient ring of a jacobson ring as jacobson ring

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
