Hom-Set as Monoid

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be locally small category and \(A \in \mathcal{C}\). Then the hom-set is a monoid

2. Proof

2.1. Neutrales Element

follows from the identity morphims

2.2. associativity

follows from associativity of the composition of morphisms
