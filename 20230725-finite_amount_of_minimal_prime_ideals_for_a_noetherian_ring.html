finite amount of minimal prime ideals for a noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring, then there exist only finitely many minimal prime ideals.

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • finite amount of irreducible components of a noetherian space
  • bijection between irreducible components and minimal prime ideals
