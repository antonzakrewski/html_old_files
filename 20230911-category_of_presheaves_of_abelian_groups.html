category of presheaves of abelian groups

1. Definition

Let \(\mathrm{Ab}\) be the category of abelian groups and \((X, \mathcal{T})\) a topological space. Then the category of presheaves of abelian groups on \(X\), \(\mathrm{PAb}(X)\) is defined with

  • Ab-valued presheaf as objects
  • morphisms of presheafs as morphisms
