hausdorff image of a compact, second countable space as second countable space

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T})\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, such that

  1. \(X\) is compact
  2. \(X\) is secondcountable
  3. \(Y\) is Hausdorff-Raum

and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a map. Then \(f[Y]\) is also second countable
