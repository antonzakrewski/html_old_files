zariski topology for a PID as T1-Space

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) be the zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) is a T1-Space
  2. \(A\) is a principal ideal domain

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
