boolean ring and quotient for a prime ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a Boolean ring, \(\mathfrak{p} \subsetneq R\) a prime ideal. Then

\begin{align*} R / \mathfrak{p} \simeq \mathbb{Z} / 2\mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \(r \in R\), then

\begin{align*} r^2 =& r \\ r (r - 1) =& 0 \end{align*}

since \(\mathfrak{p}\) is prime, we conclude

\begin{align*} r \in R \lor r - 1 \in R \end{align*}
