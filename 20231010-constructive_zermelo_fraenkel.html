Constructive Zermelo Fraenkel

1. Definition

Constructive Zermelo Fraenkel Set Theory uses intuitionistic logic and following set theoretic axioms:

  • Axiom of extensionality
  • Axiom of empty set
  • Axiom of pairing
  • Axiom of union
  • Axiom of infinity
  • Axiom schema of specification
  • Axiom schema of replacement
