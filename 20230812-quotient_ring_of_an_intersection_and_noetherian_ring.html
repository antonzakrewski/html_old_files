quotient ring of an intersection and noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(M_1,M_2 \subseteq M\) submodules such that \(M/M_1, M/M_2\) are noetherian ring. Then

\begin{align*} M/(M_1 \cap M_2) \end{align*}

is also noetherian

2. Proof
