contractible space and identity map

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space. TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is contractible space
  2. the identity map is homotopic to a constant map,

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
