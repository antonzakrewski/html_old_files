presheaf on a topological space

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathrm{Op}(X)\) the category of Open Subsets. A presheaf on \(x\) is contravariant functor to the category set

\begin{align*} F: \mathcal{T} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set} \end{align*}
