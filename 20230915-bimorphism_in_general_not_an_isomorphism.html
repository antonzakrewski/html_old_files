bimorphism in general not an isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\) a bimorphism. Then \(f\) is in general not an isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • isomorphism as injective map
  • bimorphism in general not injective
