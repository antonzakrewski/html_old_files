exact sequence and finitely generated module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M_1,M_2,M_3\) \(R\)-modules, where \(M_1,M_3\) are finitely generated modules. Suppose

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{}& M_1 \arrow[]{r}{} & M_2 \arrow[]{r}{} & M_3 \arrow[]{r}{} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

is exact. Then \(M_2\) is also finitely generated
