indiscrete space functor

1. Proposition

The indiscrete Space functor from category set to category Top is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ind}: \mathrm{Set} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ X \mapsto& (X, \{\mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{ind}}\} \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f: (X, \{\mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{ind},X}\}) \rightarrow (Y, \{\mathcal{T}_{\mathrm{ind},Y}\})) \end{align*}
