trivial valuation of a field

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(F\) be a field and \(0\) the zerogroup Then the trivial valuation of \(F\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \nu: F \rightarrow& 0 \scqup \{\infty\} \\ \alpha \mapsto \begin{cases} \infty & \mbox{if } \alpha = 0 \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
