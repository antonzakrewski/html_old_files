neutral element uniquely defined by left/right neutral element

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group and \(l \in G\) a left-neutral element (or analog right-neutral element Then

\begin{align*} e = l \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \(g \in G\), then

\begin{align*} g \cdot l =& g \cdot (l \cdot e) \\ =& g \cdot e \\ =& g \end{align*}
