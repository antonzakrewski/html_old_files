Extension in an abelian category

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be an abelian category, \(A,B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\). An Extension \(E \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) of \(B\) by \(A\) is defined as short exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & A \arrow[]{r}{} & E \arrow[]{r}{} & B \arrow[]{r}{} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
