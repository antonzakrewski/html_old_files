algebraic set of a projective space

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be a field and \(\mathbb{P}_F^n\) the projective space over \(F\). Then \(X \subseteq \mathbb{P}_F^n\) is said to be algebraic, if there exist homogeneous polynomials \(f_i \in F[X_0,X_1,...,X_n]\) such that

\begin{align*} X = \{[a_0 : ... : a_n] \in \mathbb{P}_F^n \vert f_i (a_0,...,a_n) = 0 \forall i \in I ]\} \end{align*}
