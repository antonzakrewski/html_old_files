presheaf kernel

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{A}\) an abelian category, \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) A-valued presheaves and \(\varphi: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) a morphism of presheaves Then the presheaf kernel \(\mathrm{ker}( \varphi)\) is defined as presheaf with

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ker}(\varphi)(U) \coloneqq \mathrm{ker}(\varphi_{\vert U}) \end{align*}

and the induced restriction

\begin{align*} \mathrm{res}_{\mathrm{ker}(\varphi)}^{U,V} \coloneqq \mathrm{res}^{U,V}_{\vert \mathrm{ker}(\varphi_U)} \end{align*}
