Proj scheme as locally ringed space

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring and \((\mathrm{Proj}, \mathcal{O}_{\mathrm{Proj}})\) the Proj scheme. Then \((\mathrm{Proj}, \mathcal{O}_{\mathrm{Proj}})\) is a locally ringed space
