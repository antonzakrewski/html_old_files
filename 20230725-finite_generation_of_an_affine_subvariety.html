finite generation of an affine subvariety

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) an affine space and \(V(S) \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) an affine subvariety. Then there exist finitely many \(f_1,...,f_n \in K[X_1,...,X_n]\) such that

\begin{align*} V(S) = V(f_1,...,f_n) \end{align*}
