finite generation of homogeneous components for finitely generated Algebra and Module

1. Proposition

Let (R = \bigoplusn ∈ \mathbb{N}0 Rn) be a graded ring and \(M = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0}\) a graded module. Suppose \(R\) is a finitely generated \(R_0\)-Algebra and \(M\) a finitely generated \(R\)-module. Then \(M_n\) for \(n \in \mathbb{N}_0\) is a finitely generated module
