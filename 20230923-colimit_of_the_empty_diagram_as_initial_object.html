colimit of the empty diagram as initial object

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category. Then an initial object, if it exists, is a colimit of the diagram

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \empty \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \end{align*}
