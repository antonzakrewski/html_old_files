finitely presented module and projective / flat module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(F\) a finitely presented module TFAE:

  1. \(F\) is flat
  2. \(F\) is a projective module

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
