sieve pullback

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a small category, \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(Y,X)\) \(S \in \mathrm{Sieve}(X)\) a sieve. Then the <sieve pullback> is defined as pullback in the category presheaves on

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} f^*(S) \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{} & (S: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,\mathcal{C})) \arrow[]{d}{X}\\ \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,Y) \arrow[]{r}{\circ f} & \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,X)\\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2. Concretely

for \(f: Y \rightarrow X\) and \(S \in \mathrm{Sieve4X)\) and \(Z \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) we have

\begin{align*} f^*(S)(Z) = \{\alpha: Z \rightarrow Y \vert f \circ \alpha \in S(Z)\} \end{align*}
