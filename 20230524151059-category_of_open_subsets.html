Category of Open Subsets

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, then the category of open subsets \(\mathrm{Op}(X)\) is defined as category with

  • open subsets \(O \in \mathcal{T}\) as objects
  • the inclusion as morphisms
