finitely generated A-Algebra

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(B\) an A-Algebra. Then \(A\) is said to be finitely generated, if there exist \(b_1,...,b_n \in B\) such that the evaluation homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi_{b_1,...,b_n}: A[X_1,...,X_n \twoheadrightarrow B \end{align*}

is an epimorphism
