dimension of an algebraic set and dimension of a coordinate ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be an algebraically closed field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. For an algebraic set \(Y \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\), it holds for the topological dimension and the dimension of the coordinate ring

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}(Y) =& \mathrm{dim} \mathcal{O}_{Y} \\ =& \end{align*}
