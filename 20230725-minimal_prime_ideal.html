minimal prime ideal

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathfrak{p}\) a prime ideal. Then \(\mathfrak{a}\) is a minimal prime ideal, if for a prime ideal \(\mathfrak{q}\) with

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{q} \subseteq \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}

it follows, that

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{q} = \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}
