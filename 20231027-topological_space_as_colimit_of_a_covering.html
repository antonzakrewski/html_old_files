topological space as colimit of an open covering

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(O_i\) an open covering of \(X\). Let \(\mathcal{O}\) be the full subcategory of category Top containing \(O_i\) and any finite intersection \(\bigcap_{i = 1}^n O_i\) with the subspace topology Then \(X\) is a filtered colimit of the inclusion functor \(\iota: \mathcal{O} \rightarrowtail \mathrm{Top}\)
