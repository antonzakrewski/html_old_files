Normalizer

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group and \(U \subseteq G\) a subgroup. Then the normalizer is a group defined as

\begin{align*} N_G(U) =& \{g \in G \vert gU = Ug\} \\ =& \{g \in G \vert gUg^{-1} = U\} \end{align*}
