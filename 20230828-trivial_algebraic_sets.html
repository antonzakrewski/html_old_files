trivial algebraic sets

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. Then \(\empytset, \mathbb{A}_K^n\) are algebraic sets

2. Proof

2.1. \(\emptyset\)

follows from

\begin{align*} V(K[X_1,...,X_n]) = \emptyset \end{align*}

2.2. \(\mathbb{A}_K^n\)

follows from the zero ideal

\begin{align*} V(0) = \mathbb{A}_K^n \end{align*}
