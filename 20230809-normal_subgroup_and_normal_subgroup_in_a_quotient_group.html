normal subgroup and normal subgroup in a quotient group

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(N \trianglelefteq G\) a normal subgroup and \(G/N\) the quotient group. Suppose \(U \subseteq G\) is a subgroup with \(N \subseteq U\) TFAE:

  1. \(U \trianglelefteq G\) a normal subgroup
  2. \(\pi_{N}[U] \trianglelefteq G/U\) is a normal subgroup
