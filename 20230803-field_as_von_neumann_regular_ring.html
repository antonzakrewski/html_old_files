field as von Neumann regular ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field. Then \(K\) is a von Neumann regular ring

2. Proof

Let \(a \in K\), w.l.o.g. \(a \neq 0\). Then for \(x = a^{-1}\) we get

\begin{align*} a =& a \cdot a \cdot a^{-1} \\ =& a^2 \cdot x \end{align*}
