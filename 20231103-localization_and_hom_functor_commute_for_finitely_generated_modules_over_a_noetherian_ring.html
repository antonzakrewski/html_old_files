localization and hom-functor commute for finitely generated Modules over a Noetherian Ring

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{AM_{fg}}\) be the category of finitely generated Modules for a noetherian ring \(A\). Then for the category of finitely generated Modules over a Noetherian Ring, it holds

\begin{align*} \eta: S^{-1}(\mathrm{Hom}_{A}(-,-)) () \mathrm{Hom}_{S^{-1}A}(S^{-1}(-), S^{-1}(-)) \end{align*}

see: localization and hom-functor

2. Proof

corollary:

  • finitely presented module commutes with hom-functor and localization
  • finitely generated module over a noetherian ring as finitely presented module
