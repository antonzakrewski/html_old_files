finite product of artinian modules as artinian module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M_1,M_2\) \(R\)-modules. TFAE:

  1. \(M_1,M_2\) are Artinian modules
  2. \(M_1 \oplus M_2\) is artinian

2. Proof

Consider the short exact sequence of modules

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 & M_1 & M_1 \oplus M_2 & M_2 & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
