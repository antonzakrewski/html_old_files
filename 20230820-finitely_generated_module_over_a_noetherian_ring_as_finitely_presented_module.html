finitely generated module over a noetherian ring as finitely presented module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring and \(M\) a finitely generated module. Then \(M\) is also a finitely presented module

2. Proof

By assumption, there exists a epimorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: A^m \twoheadrightarrow M \end{align*}

Furthermore, \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) \subseteq A^m\) is a submodule (cf. Kern eines Modulhomomorphismus als Untermodul) and since \(A^m\) is a noetherian module also finitely generated (see: noetherian module over a noetherian ring and finite generation)
