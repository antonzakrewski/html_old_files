presheaf image

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, \(\mathcal{F}_1,\mathcal{F}_2\) A-valued presheaf on a topological space \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\) and \(\varphi: \mathcal{F}_1 \rightarrow \mathcal{F}_2\) a morphism of sheafs Then the presheaf image of \(\varphi\) is defined as presheaf with

\begin{align*} U \mapsto \mathrm{im}(\varphi[U]) \end{align*}
