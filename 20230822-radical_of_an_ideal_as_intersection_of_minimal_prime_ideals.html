radical of an ideal as intersection of minimal prime ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathfrak{a}\) an ideal. Then the radical \(\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}}\) is the intersection of minimal prime ideals containing \(\mathfrak{a}\)

2. Proof

existence of a minimal prime ideal for a given subset, radical of an ideal as intersection of prime ideals
