contravariant Spec functor from Ring to Top

1. Definition / Proposition

Given the category CRing (non commutative ?) and category Top, the contravariant Spec Functor is defined

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}: \mathrm{CRing} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ A \mapsto& \mathrm{Spec}(A) (\varphi: A \rightarrow B) \mapsto& (\varphi': \mathrm{Spec}(B) \rightarrow \mathrm{Spec}(A)) \end{align*}

where \(\varphi^*\) is the induced continuous map between spectra
