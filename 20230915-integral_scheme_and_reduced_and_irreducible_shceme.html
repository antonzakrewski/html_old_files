integral scheme and reduced and irreducible scheme

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{O}_X, O_i)\) be a scheme

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is an integral scheme
  2. \(X\) is both reduced and irreducible

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
