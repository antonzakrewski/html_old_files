finitely generated A-Algebra over a noetherian ring is noetherian

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring and \(B\) a finitely generated A-Algebra. Then \(B\) is noetherian

2. Proof

equivalent to Hilbert's basis theorem:

  • surjective image of a finitely generated ideal
