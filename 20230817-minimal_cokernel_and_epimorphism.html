minimal cokernel and epimorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(G,H\) be groups and \(\varphi: G \rightarrow H\) a homomorphism TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is a epimorphism
  2. \(\mathrm{coker}(\varphi) = 0\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Let \(a + \mathrm{im}(\varphi) \in \mathrm{coker}(\varphi)\). Since \(\varphi\) is surjective \(a \in \mathrm{im}(\varphi)\). Hence \(a + \mathrm{im}(\varphi) = 0 + \mathrm{im}(\varphi)\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Let \(a \in H\), then by assumption \(a + \mathrm{im}(\varphi) = 0 + \mathrm{im}(\varphi)\), hence \(a \in \mathrm{im}(\varphi)\)
