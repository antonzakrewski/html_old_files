reduced polynomial

1. Definition

Let \(\mathbb{F}_p\) be the finite field and \(\mathbb{F}_p[X_i]\) the polynomial ring over \(\mathbb{F}_p\). Then a polynomial \(f \in \mathbb{F}_p\) is said to be reduced, if for every monomial

\begin{align*} \alpha \prod_{i \in I} X_i^{\nu_i} \end{align*}

it holds, that \(\nu_i < p\)
