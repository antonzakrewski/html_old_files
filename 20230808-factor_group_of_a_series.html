factor group of a series

1. Definition

Let \(G\) be a group and

\begin{align*} 1 \triangleleft G_n \triangleleft ... \triangleleft G_0 = G \end{align*}

a subnormal series. Then a factor group of the series is a quotient group \(G_{i+1}/G_{i}\) for \(i \in \{0,...,n-1\}\)
