smooth projective variety over an algebraicly closed field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be an algebraically closed field and \(\mathbb{P}_{K}^n\) the projective space and \(A = Z(f_1,...,f_r) \subseteq \mathbb{P}_{K}^n\) a projective variety, such that \(r\) is minimal

TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is a smooth projective variety
  2. for all \([\alpha_0 : ... : \alpha_n ] \in X\) the matrix
\begin{align*} (\frac{\partial f_i}{\partial x_j}(\alpha_0,...,\alpha_n))_{i=1,...,r \\ j = 0,...,n} \in \mathbb{C}^{r \times (n+1)} \end{align*}

has full rank \(r\)

  1. \(\forall 0 \leq j_1 < ... < j_r \leq n\)
\begin{align*} \det(\frac{\partial f_i}{\partial x_{j_k}}(a_0,...,a_n)_{i = 1,...,r; k = 1,...,r} \neq 0 \end{align*}

2. Proof
