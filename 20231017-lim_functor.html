derived lim functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{J}\) be categories, such that \(\mathcal{C}\) admits limits of the shape \(\mathcal{J}\). Then for the functor category \(\mathcal{C}^\mathcal{J}\) the derived colimit functor is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}: \mathcal{C}^\mathcal{J} \rightarrow& A \\ (\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}) \mapsto& \mathrm{lim}_{\mathcal{F}} \end{align*}
