flat module

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module Then \(M\) is said to be flat, if the tensoring functor is an exact functor

\begin{align*} (M \otimes -): \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{RMod} \\ N \mapsto& M \otimes N \\ \end{align*}
