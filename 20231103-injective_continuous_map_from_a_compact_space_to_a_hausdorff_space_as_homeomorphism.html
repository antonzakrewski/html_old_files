bijective continuous map from a compact space to a hausdorff space as homeomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T})\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, such that \(X\) is compact and \(Y\) is Hausdorff. Suppose \(f\) is a bijective, continuous map, then \(f\) is a homeomorphism
