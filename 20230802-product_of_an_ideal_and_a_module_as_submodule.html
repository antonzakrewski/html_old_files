product of an ideal and a module as submodule

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq R\) an ideal. Then

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{a}M = \{am \in M, a \in \mathfrak{a}, m \in M\} \end{align*}

is a submodule
