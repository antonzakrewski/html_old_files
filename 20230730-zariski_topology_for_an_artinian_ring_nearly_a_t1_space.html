zariski topology for an artinian ring nearly a T1-Space

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an Artinian ring and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the Zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology. Then \(\mathrm{Spec}(A) \setminus \{0\}\) is a T1-Space
