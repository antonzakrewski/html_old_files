cochain complex

1. Definition

a cochain complex is the dual notion of a chain complex.

2. Explizit

A cochain complex is a sequence of the form

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... & A_0 \arrow[]{r}{d_1}& A_1 \arrow[]{r}{d_2} & A_2 \arrow[]{r}{}& ... \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

where

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(d_i) \subseteq \mathrm{ker}(d_{i+1}) \end{align*}
