special linear group as variety

1. Propisition

Let \(K\) be a field, then the Special linear Group \(\mathrm{SL}_{K}(n)\) is an affine variety

2. Proof

\begin{align*} \mathrm{SL}_{K}(n) = \{A \in K^{n \times n} \vert \det(A) = 1\} \end{align*}

determinant as polynomial

\begin{align*} \mathrm{det}(A) - 1 \end{align*}
