relative homotopy to the empty set

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T})\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, \(f,g: X \rightarrow Y\) continuous maps. TFAE:

  1. \(f \simeq g\) (see: homotopy)
  2. \(f \simeq g \mathrm{rel} \emptyset\) (see: relative homotopy)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
