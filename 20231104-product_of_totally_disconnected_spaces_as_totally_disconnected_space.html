product of totally disconnected spaces as totally disconnected space

1. Proposition

Let \((X_i, \mathcal{T}_i)\) be a family of totally disconnected spaces. Then the product \(\prod_{i\in I}X_i\) with the product topology is also totally disconnected

2. Proof

Let \(A \subseteq \prod_{i \in I} X_i\) be a connected subset. Then for any \(i \in I\), it follows that \(\pi_i[A]\) is connected as image of a connected set. Therefore, \(\pi_i[A]\) is a singleton for \(i \in I\) and thus \(A = \{(\pi_i[A])_{i \in I}\}\) is also a singleton
