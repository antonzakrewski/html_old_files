chain complex of modules

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M_n\) \(R\)-modules for \(n \in \mathbb{Z}\). A chain complex is a sequence of modulehomomorphism and \(M_n\)

\begin{align*} ... \underrightarrow{d_{n+2}} M_{n+1} \underrightarrow{d_{n+1}} M_n \underrightarrow{d_n} M_{n-1} \underrightarrow{d_{n-1}} ... \end{align*}

such that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(d_{n+1}) \subseteq \mathrm{ker}(d_n) \end{align*}
