functor from top to the set of connected components

1. Proposition

there exists the functor from category Top to category set

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ X \mapsto& \{\pi(x)\} \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f': \pi(x) \mapsto& \pi(f(x))) \\ \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • composition of functors as functor
  • underlying-set functor
  • functor from Top to the category of totally disconnected space
