tangent space at a subset

1. Definition

Let \(M\) be a \(\mathcal{C}^1\) real differentiable manifold, and \(TM\) the tangential bundle Then for a subset \(A \subseteq M\), the tangent space at \(M\) is defined as

\begin{align*} T_{A} M \coloneqq \pi^{-1}[A] \end{align*}
