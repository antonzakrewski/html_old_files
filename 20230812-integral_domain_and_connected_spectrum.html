integral domain and connected spectrum

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain and \(\mathrm{Spec}(R)\) the zariski spectrum equipped with the Zariski topology. Then \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) is connected

2. Proof

follows from the zero ideal as prime ideal and integral domain
