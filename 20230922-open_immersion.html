open immersion

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{O}_X, (O_i)), (Y, \mathcal{O}_Y, (U_i))\) be schemes. An open immersion is a morphism of schemes \(f: X \rightarrow Y\), such that

  1. there exists an open subscheme \((O, \mathcal{O}_O, O_i')\) of \(Y\)
  2. \(f: X \rightarrow O\) is an isomorphism
