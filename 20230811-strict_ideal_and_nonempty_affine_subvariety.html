strict ideal and nonempty affine subvariety over an algebraically closed field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be an algebraically closed field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. Suppose \(S \subseteq K[X_1,...,X_n]\) is a strict ideal, then the generated affine subvariety \(V(S)\) is nonempty

2. Proof

weak Nullstellensatz
