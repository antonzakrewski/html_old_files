scientific writing: passive

1. Arguments

1.1. For Passive

  • emphasis on the things done
  • not personal
  • more objective, scientific
  • structure control

1.2. Against Passive

  • length
  • dangling modifiers
  • pompous / inpersonal
  • ambiguous actors
  • encourages abusive nominalizations
  • less direct, less clear
  • journals tend to like the active more
