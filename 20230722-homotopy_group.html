homotopy group

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space, \(I = [0,1]^n\) the n-dimensional unit cube and \(\partial I^n\) the boundary. Then the $n$-th homotopy group of \(X\) based at \(x_0\) is defined as the set of all homotopy classes of the homotopy group with the concatenation of homotopy classes
