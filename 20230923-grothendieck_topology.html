grothendieck topology

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be an essentially small category. A grothendieck topology \(\mathcal{T}\) on \(\mathcal{C}\) is a collection of covering sieves \(\mathrm{Cov}_{\mathcal{T}}(X) \subseteq \mathrm{Sieve}(X)\) for each object \(X \in \mathcal{C}\)
