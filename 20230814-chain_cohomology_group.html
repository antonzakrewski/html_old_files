chain cohomology group

1. Definition

Let

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... & A_0 \arrow[]{r}{d_1}& A_1 \arrow[]{r}{d_2} & A_2 \arrow[]{r}{}& ... \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

be a cochain complex. Then the $n$-th cohomology group is defined as as quotient group

\begin{align*} H_n \coloneqq \mathrm{ker}(d_n) / \mathrm{im}(d_{n+1}) \end{align*}
