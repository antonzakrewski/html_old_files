localization of a module

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(S \subseteq R\) a multiplicatively closed set. Then the localization of \(R\) is defined as

\begin{align*} S^{-1} R \coloneqq R \times S/ ~ \end{align*}

with

\begin{align*} (m,s_1) \sim (n,s_2) :\Leftrightarrow \exists s : s(m s_2 - n s_1) = 0 \end{align*}
