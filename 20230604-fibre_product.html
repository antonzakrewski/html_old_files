fibre product

1. Definition

Let \((X_i, \mathcal{T}_i)\) be a family of topological space, \(X \coloneqq \prod_{i \in I} X_i\) the product topology and \(f_i: X_i \rightarrow X_0\) a family of continuous maps to a topological space \((X_0, \mathcal{T}_0)\) and Then the fibre product is the subspace topology on \(\{x \in X \vert f_i(\pi_i(x)) = f_j(\pi_j(x)) \forall i,j \in I\}\)
