Push-Forward (fundamental group)

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(X\), \(Y\) be topological spaces and \(x \in X\). Then the Push-Forward for the fundamental group is defined as:

\begin{align*} f_{*}: \pi_1 (X,x) \rightarrow& \pi_1(Y, f(x)) \\ [\alpha] \mapsto [f \circ \alpha] \end{align*}
