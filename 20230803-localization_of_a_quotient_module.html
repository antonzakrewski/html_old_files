localization of a quotient module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(N \subseteq M\) a submodule. Then for the localization functor it holds, that

\begin{align*} S^{-1}(M/N) \simeq S^{-1}M/S^{-1}M \end{align*}

as \(S^{-1}R\)-modules
