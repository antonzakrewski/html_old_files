bijection between prime ideals and irreducible closed subsets

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the Zariski spectrum equipped with the Zariski topology Then the map

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Spec}(A) \rightarrow& \mathcal{P}(\mathrm{Spec}(A)) \\ \mathfrak{p} \mapsto& V(\mathfrak{p}) \end{align*}

is an order reversing Bijection between \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) and the set of irreducible closed subsets
