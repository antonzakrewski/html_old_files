hom-functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a locally small category category, then the hom functor is defined as bifunctor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,-): \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \times \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ (A,B) \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B) \\ (f: A' \rightarrow A,g^{\mathrm{op}}: B \rightarrow B') \mapsto& (q: A \rightarrow B \mapsto g \circ q \circ f) \end{align*}
