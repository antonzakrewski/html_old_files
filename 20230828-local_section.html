local section

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a V-valued presheaf. A local section is a pair \((O,s)\), where \(O \subseteq X\) is open and \(s \in \mathcal{F}(O)\) is a section
