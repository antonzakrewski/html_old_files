Category

1. Definition

A category \(\mathcal{C}\) consists of

  1. A class of objects, \(\mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\)
  2. for objects \(A,B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) a class of morphisms \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\)
  3. for each object \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) an identity morphism $\mathrm{id}A ∈ \mathrm{Hom}\mathcal{C}(A,A)"a
  4. A composition of morphisms
