exponential in the power series ring for characteristic 0

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring with characteristic \(\mathrm{char}(R) = 0\). Then the exponential in the power series ring \(R[[X] ]\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{exp} \coloneqq \sum_{i=0}^{n} \frac{1}{n!}X^n \end{align*}
