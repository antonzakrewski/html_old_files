localization of a ring at a prime ideal

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq R\) a prime ideal, then the localization at \(\mathfrak{p}\) is defined as

\begin{align*} S^{-1}R \end{align*}

where \(S \coloneqq R \setminus \mathfrak{p}\)
