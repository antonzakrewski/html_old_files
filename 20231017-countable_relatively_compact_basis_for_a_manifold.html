countable, relatively compact basis for a manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a topological manifold. Then there exist a countable basis of relatively compact sets.

2. Proof

corollary of:

  1. locally euclidean space as locally compact space
  2. secondcountable and locally compact space admits conutable, relatively compact basis
