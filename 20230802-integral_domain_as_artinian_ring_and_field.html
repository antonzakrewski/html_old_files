integral domain as artinian ring and field

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain. TFAE:

  1. \(R\) is a field
  2. \(R\) is an artinian ring

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of field as artinian ring

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
