nilradical as subset of the jacobson radical

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, then it holds for the nilradical and Jacobson radical

\begin{align*} \mathcal{N}_A \subseteq \mathcal{J}_A \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Nilradical as intersection of prime ideals
  • maximal ideal as prime ideal
