category of open Subsets as cofiltered category

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{C}\) be the Category of Open Subsets on \(\mathcal{T}\). Then \(\mathcal{C}\) is a cofiltered category

2. Proof

2.1. fork

follows from fork in a thin category

2.2. lower bound

follows form \(O \cap O'\) as open set (cf. Openness of a finite intersection of open sets)

2.3. nonempty

follows from \(X\) or \(\emptyset\) as open sets
