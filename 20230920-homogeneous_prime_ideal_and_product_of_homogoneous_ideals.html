homogeneous prime ideal and product of homogoneous ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{p}\) a homogeneous ideal

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{p}\) is a homogeneous prime ideal
  2. for homogeneous ideals \(\mathfrak{a}, \mathfrak{b}\) with \(\mathfrak{a} \cdot \mathfrak{b} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\) it follows, that \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\) or \(\mathfrak{b} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
