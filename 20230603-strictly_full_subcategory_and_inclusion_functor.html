strictly full subcategory and inclusion functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{D} \subeteq \mathcal{C}\) be a subcategory. TFAE:

  • \(\mathcal{C}\) is a strictly full subcategory
  • \(\mathrm{id}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) is a essentially surjective functor0
