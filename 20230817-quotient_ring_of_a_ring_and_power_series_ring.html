quotient ring of a ring and power series ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\) an ideal and \(A [[ X] ]\) the power series ring. Then

\begin{align*} A/\mathfrak{a}[ [ X] ] \simeq A [ [ X] ]/\mathfrak{a}[ [X] ] \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let

\begin{align*} \varphi: A [ [X ] ] \rightarrow& (A/\mathfrak{a})[ [ X] ] \\ (\alpha_1,\alpha_2,...) \mapsto (\alpha_1 + \mathfrak{a}, \alpha_2 + \mathfrak{a},...) \end{align*}
