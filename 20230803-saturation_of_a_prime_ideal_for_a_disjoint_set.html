saturation of a prime ideal for a disjoint set

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(S\) multiplicatively closed and \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) a prime ideal such that \(S \cap \mathfrak{p} = \emptyset\). Then

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p}^S = \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}
