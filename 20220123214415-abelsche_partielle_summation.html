Abelsche Partielle Summation

1. Satz

Seien \(a_0,a_1,...,a_n\) und \(b_0,b_1,...,b_n\) reelle Zahlen und

\begin{align*} A_k \coloneqq \sum_{i=0}^k a_i \\ B_k \coloneqq \sum_{i=0}^k b_i \\ \text{f√ºr }k = 0,1,...,n \end{align*} \begin{align*} \sum_{k=0}^n A_kb_k = A_nb_n - \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} a_{k+1}B_k \end{align*}

2. Beweis

2.1. Induktionsanfang

\begin{align*} \sum_{k=0}^0 A_0 b_0 =& A_0 \cdot b_0 \\ A_0B_0 - \sum_{k=0}^{0-1} a_{0+1}B_k =& A_0B_0 \\ B_0 =& \sum_{i=0}^0 b_i = b_0 \\ \Rightarrow& A_0 \cdot b_0 = A_0B_0 \end{align*}

2.2. Induktionsschritt

\begin{align*} \sum_{k=0}^{n+1} A_kb_k =& \sum_{k=0}^n A_kb_k + A_{n+1}b_{n+1} \\ =& \sum_{k=0}^n A_kb_k + (A_{n} + a_{n+1})b_{n+1} \\ =& \sum_{k=0}^n A_kb_k + (A_{n} + a_{n+1})b_{n+1} \\ \end{align*} \begin{align*} A_{n+1}B_{n+1} - \sum_{k=0}^{n+1-1}a_{k+1}B_k =& A_{n+1}B_{n+1} - \sum_{k=0}^{n}a_{k+1}B_k \\ =& (A_{n} + a_{n+1})(B_{n} + b_{n+1}) - \sum_{k=0}^{n-1}(a_{k+1}B_k) - a_{n+1}B_{n} \\ =& ( A_nB_n - \sum_{k=0}^{n-1}(a_{k+1}B_k) ) + (a_{n+1}B_{n} - a_{n+1}B_{n}) + (A_{n}b_{n+1} + a_{n+1}b_{n+1}) \\ =& \sum_{k=0}^{n+1}A_kb_k = + (A_{n}b_{n+1} + a_{n+1}b_{n+1}) \end{align*}
