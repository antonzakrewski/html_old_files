exact sequence of Modules and artinian Module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M_1,M_2,M_3\) \(R\)-modules such that

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & M_1 \arrow[]{r}{f} & M_2 \arrow[]{r}{g} & M_3 \arrow[]{r}{} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

is an exact sequence TFAE:

  1. \(M_2\) is an artinian module
  2. \(M_1,M_3\) are artinian modules

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
