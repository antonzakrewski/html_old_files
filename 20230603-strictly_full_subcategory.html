strictly full subcategory

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{D} \subseteq \mathcal{C}\) be a subcategory. Then \(\mathcal{C}\) is a strictly full subcategory, if \(\mathcal{C}\) is a full subcategory and for \(a \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) there exists an \(b \in \mathcal{D}\) such that \(a\) and \(b\) are isomorphic
