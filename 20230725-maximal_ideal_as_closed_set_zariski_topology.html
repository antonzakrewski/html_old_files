maximal ideal as closed set (zariski topology)

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathcal{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) (see: zariski spectrum). TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{p}\) is a maximal ideal
  2. \(\{\mathcal{p}\}\) is closed in the Zariski topology

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
