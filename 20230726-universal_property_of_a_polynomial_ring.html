universal property of a polynomial ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be commutative rings and \(A[X_m]\) a polynomial ring. Then there exists a Bijection

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{CAlg}}(A[X_m], B) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{CAlg}}(A,B) \times \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Set}}(M,B) \end{align*}
