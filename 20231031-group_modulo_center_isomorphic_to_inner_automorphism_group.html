Group modulo center isomorphic to inner automorphism group

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(Z(G)\) its center. Then the quotient group \(G/Z(G)\) is isomorphic to the inner automorphism group \(\mathrm{Inn}(G)\)

2. Proof

\begin{align*} \mathrm{id}_{G}(x) =& \varphi(g)(x) \\ x = g \cdot x \cdot g^{-1} && \vert \cdot g \\ x \cdot g =& g \cdot x \end{align*}
