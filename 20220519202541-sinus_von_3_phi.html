Sinus von 3 phi

1. Satz

\begin{align*} \sin(3\phi) = 3\cos^2(\phi)\sin(\phi) - \sin^3(\phi) \end{align*}

2. Beweis

Imaginärteil über die Formel von de Moivre

\begin{align*} \sin(3\phi) =& Im[\cos(3\phi) + i\sin(3\phi)] \\ =& Im[e^{i3\phi}] \\ =& Im[\left(\cos(\phi) + i\sin(\phi)\right)^3] \\ =& Im[\cos^3(\phi) + 3i\cos^2(\phi) - 3\sin^2(\phi)\cos(\phi) - i\sin^3(\phi)] \\ =& Im[\cos^3(\phi) - 3\sin^2(\phi)\cos(\phi) + i(3\cos^2(\phi)\sin(\phi) - \sin^3(\phi))] =& 3\cos^2(\phi)\sin(\phi) - \sin^3(\phi) \end{align*}
