noetherian ring and stabilizing of an ascending chain

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is noetherian, as in each ideal is finitely generated
  2. every chain of ideals \(\mathcal{a}_1 \subseteq \mathcal{a}_2 ...\) stabilizes
