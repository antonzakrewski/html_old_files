preimage of a monomorphism for a noetherian module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M,N\) modules over \(R\). Suppose \(\varphi: M \rightarrowtail N\) is monomorphism and \(N\) noetherian. Then \(M\) is also noetherian

2. Proof

Let \(S \subseteq M\) be a module, then \(\varphi[S] \simeq S\) and since \(N\) is noetherian, \(\varphi[S]\) is finitely generated. Therefore, since \(\varphi_{\vert S}: S \rightarrow \varphi[S]\) is an isomorphism, \(S\) is also finitely generated
