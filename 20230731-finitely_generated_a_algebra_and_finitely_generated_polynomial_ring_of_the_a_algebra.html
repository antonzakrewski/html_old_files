finitely generated A-Algebra and finitely generated Polynomial Ring of the A-Algebra

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(B\) a finitely generated A-Algebra. Then \(B[X_1,...,X_n]\) is a finitely generated A-Algebra.

2. Proof

w.l.o.g. for \(B[Y]\), the general case follows from <polynomialring in multiple variables and inductive construction>

By assumption, there exist an evaluation epimorphism \(\varphi: A[X_1,...,X_n] \twoheadrightarrow B\). Let \(A[X_1,...,X_n,X_{1,1},...,X_{1,n}\) with

\begin{align*} \varphi'(X_{1,1}) \rightarrow \varphi(X_1) X^1 \end{align*}

then \(\varphi'\) is an epimorphism. (compute)
