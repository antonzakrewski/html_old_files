commuting in a posetal category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a thin category category. Then any diagram commutes in \(\mathcal{C}\)

2. Proof

Suppose there exists a non-commuting diagram in a category \(\mathcal{C}\), then there exist at least 2 morphisms, hence \(\mathcal{C}\) is not thin
