finite union of projective algebraic sets

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid, \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring and \(\mathrm{Proj}\) the Proj. Then for the Zariski topology on Proj it holds

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{h}_1) \cup V(\mathfrak{h}_2) = V(\mathfrak{h}_1 \cdot \mathfrak{h}_2) \end{align*}
