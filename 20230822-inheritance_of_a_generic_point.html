inheritance of a generic point

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(g \in X\) a generic point and \(A \subseteq X\) a subset with \(g \in A\). Then \(g\) is a generic point of the subspace topology \(A\)
