finite product of maximal ideals as zero product and noetherian / artinian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(m_1,...,m_n\) maximal ideals such that

\begin{align*} m_1 \cdot m_2 \cdot ... \cdot m_n = 0 \end{align*}

TFAE:

  1. \(R\) is noetherian
  2. \(R\) is artinian

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
