closed immersion

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{O}_X, (O_i)), (Y, \mathcal{O}_Y, (U_i))\) be schemes and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a morphism of schemes Then \(f\) is a closed immersion, if there exists a closed subset \(A \subseteq Y\) such that:

  1. \(f: X \rightarrow A\) is a homeomorphism
  2. the induced morphism of sheaves \(f^{#}: \mathcal{O}_Y \rightarrow f_{*} \mathcal{O}_X\) is surjective
