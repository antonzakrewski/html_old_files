existence of a prime ideal for a strict ideal_

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(S \subseteq A\) a strict ideal, then there exists a prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}\) with \(S \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\)

2. Proof

corollary of

  • maximal ideal as prime ideal
  • existence of a maximal ideal for a strict ideal
