field as additive divisible group and characteristic

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be a field.

TFAE:

  1. \((F,+)\) is a divisible group
  2. \(\mathrm{char}(F) = 0\) (see: Characteristic)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
