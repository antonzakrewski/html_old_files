jacobson ideal as nilradical for a jacobson ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a Jacobson ring. Then

\begin{align*} \mathcal{N}_A = \mathcal{J}_A \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(\subseteq\)

  • Nilradical as intersection of prime ideals
\begin{align*} \mathcal{N}_A =& \bigcap_{\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)} \mathfrak{p} \\ =& \bigcap_{\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)} \bigcap_{\mathfrak{m} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(A), \mathfrak{p} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}} \mathfrak{m} \end{align*}

2.2. \(\supseteq\)

corollary of:

  • nilradical as subset of the jacobson radical
