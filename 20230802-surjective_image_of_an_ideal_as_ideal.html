surjective image of an ideal as ideal_

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be a commutative rings, \(\mathfrak{a}\) an ideal and \(\varphi: A \twoheadrightarrow B\) a epimorphism. then \(\varphi[\mathfrak{a}]\) is also an ideal

2. Proof

2.1. abelian group

corollary of Bild eines Gruppenhomomorphismus als Untergruppe

2.2. closed

suppose \(b \in B\) and \(\varphi(a_1) \in \varphi[\mathfrak{a}]\). Then there exists an \(a_2 \in \mathfrak{a}\) such that \(\varphi(a_2) = b\). Hence

\begin{align*} b \cdot \varphi(a_1) =& \varphi(a_2) \cdot \varphi(a_1) \\ =& \varphi(a_2 \cdot a_1) \\ \end{align*}

because \(a_2 \cdot a_1 \in \mathfrak{a}\), we conclude that \(\varphi(a_2 \cdot a_1) = b \cdot \varphi(a_1) \in \varphi[\mathfrak{a}]\)
