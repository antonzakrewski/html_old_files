localization functor

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(S \subseteq R\) multiplicatively closed and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category of modules over \(R\). Then the map

\begin{align*} F: \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{S^{-1}RMod} \\ M \mapsto& S^{-1}M \\ (f: M \rightarrow N) \mapsto& (S^{-1}f: S^{-1}M \rightarrow S^{-1}N) \end{align*}

is a functor

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • localization of a module homomorphism
  • localization of a composition of module homomorphisms
