generated subgroup and smallest group

1. Satz

Let \((G, \cdot)\) be a group and \(H \subseteq G\). TFAE:

  1. \(\langle H\rangle\) is the generated subgroup
  2. \(\langle H\rangle\) is the smallest subgroup with \(H \subseteq \langle H\rangle\)

2. Beweis

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
