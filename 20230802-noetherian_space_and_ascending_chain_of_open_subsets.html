noetherian space and ascending chain of open subsets

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space. TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is a noetherian topological space
  2. each chain of open subsets satisfies the ascending chain condition

2. Proof

since the map \(\psi: U \rightarrow X \setminus U\) reverses order, 1) and 2) are equivalent
