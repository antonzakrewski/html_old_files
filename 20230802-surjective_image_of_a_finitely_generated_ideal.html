surjective image of a finitely generated ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be a commutative rings, \(\mathfrak{a}\) a finitely generated ideal and \(\varphi: A \twoheadrightarrow B\) a epimorphism. then \(\varphi[I]\) is a finitely generated ideal

2. Proof

welldefined as a consequence of Image of an epimorphism and prime ideal

Suppose \(a_1,...,a_n\) generates \(\mathfrak{a}\), Then \(\varphi(a_1),...,\varphi(a_n)\) generate \(\varphi[I]\) (todo :))
