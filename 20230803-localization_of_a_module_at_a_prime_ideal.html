localization of a module at a prime ideal

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) a prime ideal. Let \(S \coloneqq R \setminus \mathfrak{p}\) Then the localization of \(M\) behind \(\mathfrak{p}\) is defined as module localization

\begin{align*} M_{\mathfrak{p}} \coloneqq S^{-1}M \end{align*}
