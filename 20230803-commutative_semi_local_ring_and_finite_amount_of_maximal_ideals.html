commutative semi local ring and finite amount of maximal ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring. TFAE:

  1. \(R\) is a semi local ring
  2. \(R\) has only finitely many maximal ideals

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
