exact sequence and epimorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(H,G\) be groups and \(0\) the zero group and \(\varphi: H \rightarrow G\) a homomorphism TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is an epimorphism
  2. the sequence \(H \underrightarrow{\varphi} G \underrightarrow{\psi} 1\) is exact at \(G\)

2. Proof

By construction we get

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ker}(\psi) = G \end{align*}

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Since \(\varphi\) is an epimorphism, it holds that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(\varphi) =& G \\ =& \mathrm{ker}(\psi) \end{align*}

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

By assumption we get

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(\psi) =& \mathrm{ker}(\varphi) \\ =& G \end{align*}
