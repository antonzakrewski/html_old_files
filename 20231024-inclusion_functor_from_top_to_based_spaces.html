basification Functor from Top to based spaces

1. Definition

Let \(\mathrm{Top}_{*}\) be the category of based spaces and \(\mathrm{Top}\) be the category Top Then the inclusion functor is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{B}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top}_{*} \\ X \mapsto& X \sqcup \{*\} \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f_*: (X \scqup \{*\}, *) \rightarrow (Y \sqcup \{*\}, *) \\ \end{align*}
