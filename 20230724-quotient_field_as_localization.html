Quotient Field as localization

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an integral domain and \(R'\) be a commutative ring TFAE:

  1. \(R' \coloneqq Q(R)\) is the quotient field / Quotient Fied.
  2. \(R' \coloneqq S^{-1}R\) is the localization along \(S \coloneqq R \setminus \{0\}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
