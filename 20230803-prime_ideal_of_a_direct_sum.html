prime ideal of a direct sum

1. Proposition

Let \(R_i\) be rings for \(i \in I\) and

\begin{align*} \coprod R_i \end{align*}

the direct sum of rings and \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq \coprod R_i\) a prime ideal. Then there exists an prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}_j \subseteq R_j\) such that

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p} = \coprod_{i \neq j} R_i \sqcup \mathfrak{p}_j \end{align*}

2. Proof

Gedanke: multiplikation mit \((1,0,...,)\) bzw. $(0,1,…)
