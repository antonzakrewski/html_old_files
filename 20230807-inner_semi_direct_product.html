inner semidirect product

1. Definition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(H,N\) subgroups, where \(N\) is a normal subgroup. Then

\begin{align*} N \rtimes H \end{align*}

is said to be a inner semidirect product, if it is a semidirect product for

\begin{align*} \varphi: H \rightarrow& \mathrm{Aut}(H) \\ h \mapsto (n \mapsto hnh^{-1}) \end{align*}
