stabilizing chain

1. Definition

Let \((M,\leq)\) be a poset and $m1 ≤ m2 ≤ …$ a chain (or $m1 ≥ m2 …$) Then the chain is said to be stabilizing, if there exists an \(n \in \mathbb{N}\), such that

\begin{align*} m_n = m_{n+1} = m_{n+2} = ... \end{align*}
