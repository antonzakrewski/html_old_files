cospan

1. Definition

A cospan is a span in the opposite category

2. Explicite

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(A,B,C \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) A cospan from \(A\) to \(B\) is a diagram of the form

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{rd}{f} & & B \arrow[]{ld}{g}\\ & C & \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
