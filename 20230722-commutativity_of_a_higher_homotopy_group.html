commutativity of a higher homotopy group

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(x_0 \in X\) and \(\pi_n(X,x_0)\) the homotopy group for \(n \geq 2\). Then \(\pi_n(X,x_0)\) is commutative

2. Proof

Eckmann-Hilton Argument
