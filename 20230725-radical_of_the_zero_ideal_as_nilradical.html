radical of the zero ideal as nilradical

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring. Then for the radical of an ideal, the zero ideal \(0\) and the nilradical \(\mathcal{N}_A\) it holds, that

\begin{align*} \sqrt{0} = \mathcal{N}_A \end{align*}
