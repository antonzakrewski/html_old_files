1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) V-valued presheaves and \(\varphi: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) a morphism of presheaves. Then the sheaf cokernel is defined as sheafification of the presheaf image
