image of a homomorphism preserving order and kernel

1. Proposition

Let \(G,H\) be groups, \(\varphi: G \rightarrow H\) a homomorphism and \(S_1 \subsetneq S_2 \subseteq G\) subgroups. TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi[S_1] \subsetneq \varphi[S_2]\)
  2. \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) \cap S_1 S_2 \subseteq S_1\) (see: product of group subset

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
