nilpotent element in a polynomial ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(A[X]\) the polynomial ring and \(f = \sum_{i=0}^{n} a_i X^i \in A[X]\) TFAE:

  1. \(f \in \mathcal{N}_{A[X]}\) is nilpotent
  2. all coefficients \(a_i\) are nilpotent

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Let \(m\) be the minimal number, such that \(a_i^m = 0\) for \(i \in \{1,..,n\}\). Then by the Pigeonhole principle, we conclude \(f^{n \cdot m} = 0\) (todo)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

corollary of:

  • Summe von nilpotenten Elementen sind nilpotent
