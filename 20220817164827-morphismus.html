morphism

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(A, B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\). A morphism \(f: A \rightarrow B\) is an object - most often a map - which satisfies following properties:

  • composition of morphisms
  • associaciativity of the composition
  • identity morphism
