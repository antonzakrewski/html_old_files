equivalence of left- and right-splitting for abelian groups

1. Proposition

Let

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & A_1 \arrow[hookrightarrow,]{r}{\varphi_1} & A_2 \arrow[twoheadrightarrow]{r}{\varphi_2}& A_3 \arrow[]{r}{\varphi_3} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

be a short exact sequence of abelian groups. TFAE:

  1. there exists a left-splitting \(\psi_1 \circ \varphi_1 = \mathrm{id}_{A_i}\)
  2. there exists a right-splitting \(\varphi_2 \circ \psi_2 = \mathrm{id}_{A_3}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
