integral A-Algebra

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a ring and \(B\) an A-Algebra. Then \(B\) is said to be integral over \(A\) if for every element \(b \in B\) there exists a monic polynomial \(f \in A[X]\) such that for the evaluation homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi_b(f) = 0 \end{align*}
