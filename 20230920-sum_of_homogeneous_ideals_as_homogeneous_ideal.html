sum of homogeneous ideals as homogeneous ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring. Then for homogeneous ideals \(\mathfrak{h}_i\), the sum of ideals

\begin{align*} \sum_{i \in I} \mathfrak{h}_i \end{align*}

is also homogeneous

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • homogeneous ideal and generation by homogeneous elements
  • generated ideal and sum
