inclusion functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{D}\) a subcategory. Then the inclusion functor is defined as covariant functor

\begin{align*} \mathcal{i}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow& \mathcal{D} \\ A \mapsto& A \\ (f: A \rightarrow B) \mapsto& (f: A \rightarrow B) \end{align*}
