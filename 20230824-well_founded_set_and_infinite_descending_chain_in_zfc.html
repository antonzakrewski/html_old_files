well-founded set and infinite descending chain in ZFC

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a set in ZFC and \(R\) a binary relation. TFAE:

  1. \(R\) is well-founded
  2. there exists no infinite descending chain

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of well-founded set implies no infinite descending chain

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

corollary of Zorn's Lemma, as every inductive chain
