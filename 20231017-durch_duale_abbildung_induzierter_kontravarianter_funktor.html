durch duale Abbildung induzierter kontravarianter Funktor

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathrm{Vect_K}\) the category vectk. Then the transpose induces a contravariant functor

\begin{align*} ^*: \mathrm{Vect_K} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Vect_K} \\ W \mapsto& W^* \\ (f: V \rightarrow W) \mapsto& (f^*: W^* \rightarrow V^*) \end{align*}
