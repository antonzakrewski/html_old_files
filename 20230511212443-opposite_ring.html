opposite ring

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \((R,+,\cdot)\) a ring, then the opposite ring \(R^{\mathrm{op}} = (R,+,*)\) is defined with:

\begin{align*} *: R \times R \rightarrow& R \\ (r,r') \mapsto r' \cdot r \end{align*}
