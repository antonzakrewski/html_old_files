Beweis: Kreiswinkelsatz: Mittelpunkt innerhalb des Dreiecks

1. Beweis

o.B.d.A. für \(\angle ACB\):

\begin{tikzpicture} \draw[] (0,0) circle (1cm); \coordinate (a) at (350:1cm); \coordinate (b) at (70:1cm); \coordinate (c) at (200:1cm); \coordinate (m) at (0,0); \node[right] (A) at (a) {A}; \node[above] (B) at (b) {B}; \node[left] (C) at (c) {C}; \draw (a) -- (b) -- (c) -- cycle; \node[above] (M) at (m) {M}; \foreach \n in {a,b,c,i} \node at (\n)[circle, fill, inner sep=1pt]{}; \draw (m) -- (a); \draw (m) -- (b); \draw[dashed] (m) -- (c); \end{tikzpicture}

Aus den gleichseitigen Dreiecken \(\triangle BCM\) und \(\triangle ACM\) folgt:

\begin{align*} m\angle CBM =& m\angle MCB \\ m\angle MAC =& m\angle ACM \end{align*}

Für die Dreicke \(\triangle CBA\) und \(\triangle BMA\) gilt aufgrund der Winkelsumme im Dreieck_

\begin{align*} 180^\circ =& m\angle MBA + m\angle BAM + m\angle AMB \\ 180^\circ =& m\angle MBA + m\angle BAM + m\angle ACB + 2 \cdot (m\angle MCB + m\angle ACM) \\ \Rightarrow m\angle AMB =& 2\cdot (m\angle MCB + m\angle ACM) \\ \Rightarrow m\angle AMB =& 2 \cdot m\angle ACB \end{align*}
