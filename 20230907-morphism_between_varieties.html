morphism of varieties

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(X,Y\) varieties. A morphism of varieties \(\varphi: X \rightarrow Y\) is a continuous map, such that for every open set \(V \subseteq Y\) and every regular function \(f: V \rightarrow K\) the composition

\begin{align*} f \circ \varphi: \varphi^{-1}[V] \rightarrow& K \end{align*}

is a regular function
