category of finitely generated Modules over a Noetherian Ring

1. Definition

The category of finitely generated Modules over a Noetherian Ring, \(\mathrm{AMod_{fg}}\), contains

  1. finitely generated modules over the noetherian ring \(A\) as objects
  2. module-homomorphisms as morphisms
