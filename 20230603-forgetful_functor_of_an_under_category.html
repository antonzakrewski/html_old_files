forgetful functor of an under category

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(X \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) and \(X/\mathcal{C}\) the under category. Then the canonical forgetful functor \(p_X: X/\mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) is defined as:

\begin{align*} F: (X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& Y \\ (g: (X \rightarrow Y) \rightarrow& (X \rightarrow Y')) \mapsto& g: Y \rightarrow Y' \end{align*}
