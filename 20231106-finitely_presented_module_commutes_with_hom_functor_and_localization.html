finitely presented module commutes with hom-functor and localization

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(S \subseteq R\) a multiplicatively closed subset \(M\) a finitely presented module. Then for any module \(N\), it holds that the localization functor and hom-functor commute up to isomorphism, i.e

\begin{align*} S^{-1} \mathrm{Hom}_{R}(M,N) \simeq \mathrm{Hom}_{S^{-1}R}(S^{-1}M, S^{-1}M) \end{align*}
