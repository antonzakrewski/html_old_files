uniform structure

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a set. A uniform structure \(\Phi\) on \(X\) is a a nonempty collection \(\Phi \subseteq \mathcal{P}(X \times X)\) such that:

  • for \(U \in \Phi\) it follows, that \(\Delta \subseteq U\), where \(\Delta = \{(x,x) \vert x \in X\}\)
  • for \(U \in \Phi\) and \(U \subseteq V \subseteq X \times X\), it follows, that \(V \in \Phi\)
  • \(\Phi\) is closed under finite intersections
