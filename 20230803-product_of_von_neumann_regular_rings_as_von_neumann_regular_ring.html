product of von neumann regular rings as von neumann regular ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R_i\) be von Neumann regular rings and \(R \coloneqq \prod R_i\) the direct product of modules. Then \(R\) is von Neumann regular.

2. Proof

Let \(a = (a_i)\). Then for each \(i \in I\) there exists an \(x_i \in X\) such that

\begin{align*} a_i = a_i \cdot x \cdot a_i \end{align*}

Hence for \(x = (x_i)\) we get

\begin{align*} a =& (a_i) \\ =& a x \cdot a \end{align*}
