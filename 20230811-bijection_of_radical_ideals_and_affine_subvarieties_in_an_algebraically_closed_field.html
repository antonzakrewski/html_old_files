Bijection of radical ideals and affine subvarieties in an algebraically closed field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be an algebraically closed field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space \(\mathcal{V}\) the set of affine subvarieties, \(K[X_1,...,X_n]\) the polynomial ring and \(\mathcal{R}\) the set of radical ideals Then the maps

\begin{align*} \mathcal{I}: \mathcal{V} \rightarrow& \mathcal{R} \\ V \mapsto& \mathcal{I}(V) \\ V: \mathcal{R} \rightarrow& \mathcal{V} \\ \mathfrak{a} \mapsto& V(\mathfrak{a}) \end{align*}

are inverse, order reversing bijections
