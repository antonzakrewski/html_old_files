prime ideal in a ring and induced prime ideal in the power series ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(A [ [ X] ]\) the power series ring and \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq A\) a prime ideal. Then

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p}[ [ X]] \end{align*}

is a prime ideal in \(A [ [ X] ]\)

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • quotient ring of a ring and power series ring
  • prime ideal and quotient ring as integral domain
  • power series ring and integral domain
