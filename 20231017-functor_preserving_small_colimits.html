Functor preserving small colimits

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is said to preserve limits, if every small colimit is preserved under \(\mathcal{F}\)
