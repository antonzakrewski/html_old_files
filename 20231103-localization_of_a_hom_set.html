localization and hom-functor

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(S \subseteq A\) a multiplicatively closed subset and \(\mathrm{AMod}\) the category RMod.

Then for the localization functor for modules

\begin{align*} \mathrm{S^{-1}}(-) \mathrm{AMod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{S^{-1}AMod} \\ A \mapsto& S^{-1}A \end{align*}

and the hom-functor it

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{R}(-, -): \mathrm{RMod}^{\mathrm{op}} \times \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{RMod} \\ (M,N) \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,N) \end{align*}

there exists natural transformation

\begin{align*} \eta: S^{-1} (\mathrm{Hom}_{A}(-,-)) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{S^{-1}A}(S^{-1}(-), S^{-1}(-)) S^{-1} \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(M,N) \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{S^{-1}A}(S^{-1}M,S^{-1}N) \end{align*}
