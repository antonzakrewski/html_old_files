lower semicontinuous function

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(f: X \rightarrow \overline{\mathbb{R}}\) be a function. Then \(f\) is said to be lower semicontinuous, if for each \(a \in \mathbb{R}\) the set

\begin{align*} \{x \in X \vert f(x) > a\} \end{align*}

is open
