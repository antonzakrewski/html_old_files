morphism of presheaves induces morphism on the stalks

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}\) V-valued presheafs for a category \(\mathcal{V}\) with stalks \(\mathcal{F}_p,\mathcal{G}_p\) at a point \(p \in X\). Suppose \(f: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) is a morphism of presheaves Then \(f\) induces a unique morphism \(f: \mathcal{F}_p \rightarrow \mathcal{G}_p\)

2. Proof

Let \(U \subseteq V \subseteq X\), with \(p \in U,V\) then it induces a unique morphism

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(V) \arrow[]{rr}{\mathrm{res}} \arrow[]{rd}{\varphi_V} & & \mathcal{F}(U) \arrow[]{ld}{\varphi_V}\\ & \mathcal{F}_p & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Furthermore, we get following commuting diagram

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(V) \arrow[bend right]{dd}{f} \arrow[]{rr}{\mathrm{res}} \arrow[swap]{rd}{\varphi_V} & & \mathcal{F}(U) \arrow[bend left]{dd}{f} \arrow[]{ld}{\varphi_U}\\ & \mathcal{F}_p & \\ \mathcal{G}(U) \arrow[]{rr}{\mathrm{res}} & & \mathcal{G}(V) \\ & \mathcal{G}_p & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

where by \(f\) being a morphism of presheafs, we it follows by definition, that following diagram commutes

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(V) \arrow[]{d}{f} \arrow[]{r}{\mathrm{res}}& \mathcal{F}(U) \arrow[]{d}{f}\\ \mathcal{G}(V) \arrow[]{r}{\mathrm{res}}& \mathcal{G}(U) \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Therefore, by definition of \(\mathcal{G}_p\) we conculde, that

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(V) \arrow[swap, bend right=90, thick]{ddr}{f \circ \psi_{V}} \arrow[]{d}{f} \arrow[thick]{rr}{\mathrm{res}}& & \mathcal{F}(U) \arrow[bend left=90, thick]{ddl}{f \circ \psi_{U}} \arrow[]{d}{f}\\ \mathcal{G}(V) \arrow[]{rd}{\psi_V} \arrow[]{rr}{\mathrm{res}}& & \mathcal{G}(U) \arrow[]{ld}{\psi_U} \\ & \mathcal{G}_p & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

commutes, hence by universal property of \(\mathcal{F}_p\), there exists a unique morphism

\begin{align*} f^*: \mathcal{F}_p \rightarrow \mathcal{G}_p \end{align*}
