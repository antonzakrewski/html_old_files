monomorphism for a quotient of a preimage

1. Proposition

Let \(G,G'\) be groups, \(H \subseteq G'\) a normal subgroup and \(\varphi: G \rightarrow G'\) a homomorphism. Then the map

\begin{align*} \varphi': G/\varphi^{-1}[H] \rightarrowtail& G'/H \\ g + \varphi^{-1}[H] \mapsto& \varphi(g) + H \end{align*}

is a monomorphism see: quotient group
