diffeomorphism for Manifolds

1. Definition

Let \(M,N\) be \(\mathcal{C}^k\) differentiable manifold. A map \(f: M \rightarrow N\) is said to be a diffeomorphism, if

  1. \(f\) is bijective
  2. \(f\) is differentiable
  3. \(f^{-1}\) is also differentiable
