preserved filtered colimit

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, \(L\) a filtered colimit for the filtered diagram \(\mathcal{J}: J \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a functor Then \(L\) is said to be preserved under \(\mathcal{F}\), if \(\mathcal{F}(L)\) is a filtered colimit for the filtered diagram \(\mathcal{F} \circ \mathcal{J}: J \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\)
