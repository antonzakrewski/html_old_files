submodule of a noetherian module as noetherian module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) a noetherian module over \(R\). Suppose \(N \subseteq M\) is a submodule, then \(N\) is also noetherian

2. Proof

Let \(S \subseteq N\) be a submodule, then \(S \subseteq M\) is also a submodule. Hence \(S\) is finitely generated.
