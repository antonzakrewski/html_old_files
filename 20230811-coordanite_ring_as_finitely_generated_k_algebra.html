coordinate ring as finitely generated K-Algebra

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(V \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) an affine subvariety. Then the coordinate ring is an finitely generated K-Algebra.d

2. Proof

follows from the definition

\begin{align*} \mathcal{O}(V) = K[X_1,...,X_n]/\mathcal{I}(V) \end{align*}
