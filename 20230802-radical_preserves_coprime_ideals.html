radical preserves coprime ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathfrak{a}_1,\mathfrak{a}_2\) ideals. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{a}_1,\mathfrak{a}_2\) are coprime
  2. \(\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_1},\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_2}\) are coprime

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of

  • coprime and bigger ideal
  • radical of an ideal and the ideal

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

corollary of

  • radical of ideals and sum
  • radical as Ring
\begin{align*} R =& \sqrt{R} \\ =& \sqrt{\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_1} + \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_2}} \\ =& \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}_1 + \mathfrak{a}_2} \\ =& \mathfrak{a}_1 + \mathfrak{a}_2 \end{align*}
