Immersion

1. Definition

Let \(M,N\) be differentiable manifolds and \(f: M \rightarrow N\) a <differentiable> map Then \(f\) is said to be an Immersion, if

\begin{align*} D_pf: T_pM \rightarrow T_{f(p)}N \end{align*}
