functor from Set to the indiscrete topology

1. Definition / Proposition

Given the category set, the functor \(\mathrm{indisc}\) to the category Top is defined by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{disc}: \mathrm{Set} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ X \mapsto& (X, \{X, \emptyset\}) \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f: (X, \{X, \emptyset\}) \rightarrow (Y, \{X, \emptyset\})) \end{align*}
