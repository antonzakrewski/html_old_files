\begin{align*} x_1 =& \frac{-b + \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \\ x_2 =& \frac{-b - \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} x_1 =& x_2 \Rightarrow \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac} = 0 \\ \Rightarrow x =& \frac{-b}{2a} \end{align*}

2.2. Ableitungsfunktion

\begin{align*} f'(x) =& 2ax + b \\ =& 0 \\ 2ax =& - b \\ x =& -\frac{b}{2a} \end{align*}
