algebraically independent

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(B\) an A-Algebra and \(T \subseteq B\). Then \(T\) is said to be algebraically independent, if the evaluation homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: A[X_t \vert t \in T] \rightarrow& B \end{align*}

is a monomorphism
