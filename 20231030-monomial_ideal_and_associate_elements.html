monomial ideal and division

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring and \(A[X_i]\) the polynomial ring and \(\mathfrak{a} = \langle X^{\alpha} \rangle \subseteq A[X_1,...,X_n]\) a monomial ideal.7 Suppose \(X^{\beta}\) is a monomial

TFAE:

  1. \(X^{\beta} \in \mathfrak{a}\)
  2. \(X^{\alpha} \mid x^{\beta}\) for a generating monomial \(X^{\alpha}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

by assumption, there exist \(\alpha(i)\) such that

\begin{align*} X^{\beta} = \sum_{i=0}^{n} h_i X^{\alpha(i)} \end{align*}

for polynomials \(h_i \in A[X_i]\). Therefore, each summand \(h_i \cdot X^{\alpha(i)}\) is divisible by \(X^{\alpha(i)}\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from the definition
