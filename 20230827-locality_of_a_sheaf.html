locality of a sheaf

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a V-valued sheaf. Suppose \(U \subseteq X\) is open, \(U_i \subseteq U\) a cover of \(U\) and \(s,t \in \mathcal{F}(U)\) sections such that

\begin{align*} s_{\vert U_i} = t_{\vert U_i} \forall i \in I \end{align*}

Then \(s = t\)
