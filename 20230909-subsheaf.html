subsheaf

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a V-valued sheaf. A subsheaf \(\mathcal{F}' \subseteq \mathcal{F}\) is a sheaf, such that for any open subset \(O \subseteq X\) it follows, that \(\mathcal{F}'(O) \subseteq \mathcal{F}\) is a Subobject and that the restriction morphism of \(\mathcal{F}'\) are induced by \(\mathcal{F}\)
