differentiable Manifold uniquely defined by covering and transition maps up to diffeomorphism

1. Proposition

Given a differentiable manifold \(M\) with a differentiable Atlas \((U_i, \varphi_i)\), we can reconstruct \(M\) up to Diffeomorphism just from the \(\varphi_{i}(U_i)\) together with the structure cocyle given by the transition maps \(f_{1,2}\)

2. Proof

\(M\) a smooth Manifold, \(\mathcal{A} = \{(U_i, \varphi_i) \vert i \in I\}\) a differentiable atlas. All the \(\varphi_i\) take values in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) \(n = \mathrm{dim}(M)\). consider triples \((x,i,v) \in M \times I \times \mathbb{R}^n\) with \(x \in U_i\) On the set of such triples, define the relation \((x,i,v) \simeq (y,j,w)\) by \(x = y\) and
