q-frobenius endomorphism as identity for a finite field

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{F}_q\) be a finite field with \(\vert \mathbb{F}_q \vert = q\). Then the q-frobenius endomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathbb{F}_q \rightarrow& \mathbb{F}_q \\ x \mapsto x^q \end{align*}

2. Proof

Satz von Fermat (Gruppentheorie)
