projective subvariety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{P}_{K}^n\) the projective space and \(A \subseteq \mathbb{P}_{K}^n\) projective variety. Then a projective subvariety is a subvariety \(A \subseteq \mathbb{P}_K^n\)
