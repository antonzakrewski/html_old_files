ring of regular functions

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(X \subseteq \mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) be an affine variety. Then the ring of regular functions on \(X\) is defined as set of regular functions with pointwise defined addition and multiplication
