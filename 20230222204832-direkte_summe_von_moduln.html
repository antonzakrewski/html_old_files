interne direkte Summe von Moduln

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(N_i \subseteq M\) submodules Then \(N_i\) is a internal direct sum, if the homomorphism

\begin{align*} \coprod N_i \rightarrow& M \\ (n_i) \mapsto \sum_{i \in I} n_i \end{align*}

is an isomorphism
