conjugation as subgroup of automorphism group

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(C \subseteq \mathrm{Aut}(G)\) the subset of conjugation automorphisms. Then \(C\) is a subgroup.

2. Proof

2.1. identity

follows from conjugation with \(e\)

2.2. closed

follows from

\begin{align*} \varphi_g \circ \varphi_h(x) =& \varphi_g ( h \cdot x \cdot h^{-1}) \\ =& g \cdot h \cdot x \cdot h^{-1} g^{-1} \\ =& \end{align*}
