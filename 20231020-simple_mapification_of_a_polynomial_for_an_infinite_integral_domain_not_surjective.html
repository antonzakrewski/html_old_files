simple mapification of a polynomial for an infinite integral domain not surjective

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain with \(\vert R \vert = \infty\), \(R[X_i]\) the polynomial ring and

\begin{align*} f: R[X_i] \rightarrow \mathrm{Maps}(\prod X_i \mapsto R) \end{align*}

the mapification Then \(f\) is not surjective
