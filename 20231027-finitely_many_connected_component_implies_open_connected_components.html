finitely many connected component implies open connected components

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, such that \(X\) has only finitely many connected components. Then each connected component is open

2. Proof

follows from:

  • Zusammenhangskomponente als abgeschlossene Menge
  • finite union of closed sets
