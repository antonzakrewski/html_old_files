finitely presented module as flat module and free localization

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(F\) a finitely presented module TFAE:

  1. \(F\) is flat
  2. \(F_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is a free module over \(R_{\mathfrak{p}}\) for any prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(R)\)
  3. \(F_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is a free module over \(R_{\mathfrak{p}}\) for any maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(R)\)
