1. Satz

\begin{align*} (a_1 + a_2 + ... + a_n) (b_1 + b_2 + ... + b_k) =& \\ =& (\sum_{i=1}^n a_i) (\sum_{j=1}^k b_j) \\ =& \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^k a_ib_j \end{align*}
