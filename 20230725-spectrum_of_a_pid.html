spectrum of a PID

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a principal ideal domain, then the zariski spectrum is

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}(R) = \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(A) \sqcup \{0\} \end{align*}
