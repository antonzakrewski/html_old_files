bimorphism in general not split epi

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\) a bimorphism. Then \(f\) is in general not split epi

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • bimorphism in general not surjective
  • split epi as surjective map
