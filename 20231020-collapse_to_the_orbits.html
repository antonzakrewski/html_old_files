orbital collapse

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(G\) a group and \(\triangle: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a (topo ?) group action. Then the collopse to the orbits is defined as final topology on

\begin{align*} G/\sim \end{align*}

where \(\sim\) is the equivalence relation of an orbit
