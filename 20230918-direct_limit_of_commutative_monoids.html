direct limit of commutative Monoids

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{Mon}\) be the category of commutative monoids and \(F: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be a filtered diagram. Then the direct limit of \(F\) is

\begin{align*} M \coloneqq (\coprod_{i \in J} M_i)/~ \end{align*}

where

\begin{align*} (m,i) \sim (n,j) :\Leftrightarrow \exists k \in J f_{ik}(m) = f_j{jk}(n) \end{align*}

and multiplication is defined

\begin{align*} [(m,i)][(n,j)] = [f_{ik}(m)f_{jk}(n)] \end{align*}
