graded subgroup

1. Definiiton

Let \(G \simeq \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z}}\) be a graded abelian group and \(A \subseteq G\) a subgroup. Then \(A\) is a graded subgroup, if \(A\) is itself graded \(A \simeq \bioplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z} } A_n\) and \(A_n \subseteq C_n\) for \(n \in \mathbb{Z}\)
