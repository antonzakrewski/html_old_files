subbasis and second countability

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space.

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is secondcountable
  2. there exists a countable Subbasis

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
