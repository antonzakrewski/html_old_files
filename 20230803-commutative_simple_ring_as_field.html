commutative simple ring as field

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative simple ring. Then \(R\) is a field

2. Proof

By assumption the zero ideal \(0\) is the maximal ideal. Therefore \(R/0\) is a field (see: maximales Ideal eines kommutativen Rings und Faktorring als KÃ¶rper) Furthermore

\begin{align*} R/0 \simeq R \end{align*}

Hence \(R\) is a field
