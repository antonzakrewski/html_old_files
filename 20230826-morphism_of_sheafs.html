morphism of sheaves

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) a V-valued sheaf on \(X\). Then a morphism of sheafs \(f: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) is a morphism of presheafs \(f: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\)
