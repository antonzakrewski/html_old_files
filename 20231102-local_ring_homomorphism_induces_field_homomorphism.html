local ring homomorphism induces field homomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be local rings and \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) a local ring homomorphism. Then for the quotient fields for the maximal ideal \(A/\mathfrak{m}, B/\mathfrak{n}\), there exists a canonical field-homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi': A/\mathfrak{m} \rightarrow B/\mathfrak{n} \end{align*}
