composition pushforward

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a small category, \(A,X,Y \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) objects and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a morphism Then for the hom-sets \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(X,A), \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(Y,A)\) the composition pushforward morphism \(f^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,X) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,Y)\) is defined as

\begin{align*} f^{ \leftarrow}(\alpha) = f \circ \alpha \end{align*}
