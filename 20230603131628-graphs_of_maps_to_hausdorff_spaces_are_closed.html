Graphs of maps to Hausdorff spaces are closed

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\) be a topological space and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be a Hausdorff-Space. Suppose \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) be a continuous map, then the Graph is closed in \(X \times Y\)
