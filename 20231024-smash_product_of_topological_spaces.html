smash product of topological spaces

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T}_X, x_0)\) and \((Y, \mathcal{T}_Y, y_0)\) be based spaces. Then the smash product \(X \ Y\) is defined as collapse

\begin{align*} X \wedge Y \coloneqq \left(X \times Y \right) / \left(X \vee Y \right) \end{align*}

for the wedge product \(X \vee Y\)
