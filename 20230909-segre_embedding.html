segre embedding

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{P}^n_K, \mathbb{P}^m_K\) projective spaces. Then the segre embedding is defined as map

\begin{align*} \sigma: \mathbb{P}_K^n \times \mathbb{P}_K^m \rightarrow& \mathbb{P}^{(n+1)(m+1)-1} \\ ([X_0 : ... : X_n],[Y_0,...,Y_m] \mapsto& [X_0Y_0 : X_0Y_1 : ... X_1Y_0 : ... : X_iY_j : ... : X_nY_m] \end{align*}
