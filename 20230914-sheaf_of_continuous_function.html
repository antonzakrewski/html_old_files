Let \((X,\mathcal{T})\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces. Then the sheaf of continuous functions from \(X\) to \(Y\) is defined through

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(U) =& C(U,Y) \end{align*}

with the restriction

\begin{align*} r_{V \cap U}(f: U \rightarrow Y) = f_{\vert V \cap U}: V \cap U \rightarrow Y \end{align*}
