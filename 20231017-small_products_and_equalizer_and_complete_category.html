small products and equalizer and complete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{C}\) is complete
  2. there exist small products and equalizers

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case, see
