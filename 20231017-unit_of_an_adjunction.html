unit of an adjunction

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{L}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{R}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be functors such that \(\mathcal{L} \dashv \mathcal{R}\) are adjoint. Then the unit of the adjunction is defined as natural transformation

\begin{align*} \eta: \mathrm{id}_{\mathcal{C}} \rightarrow R \circ L \end{align*}
