mapping torus over a differentiable manifold as differentiable Manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a real differentiable manifold of dimension \(m\) with an Atlas \(\mathcal{A} = (\varphi_{i}, U_i, (\varphi_{i,j}}))\) and \(\psi: M \rightarrow M\) a diffeomorphism. Then the mapping torus \(M_{\varphi}\) on \(M\) is an \(m + 1\) dimensional differentiable manifold for the charts

\begin{align*} \varphi'_{i,s}: \pi[U_1 \times (- \frac{1}{3} + s, \frac{1}{3} + s] \rightarrow& \varphi[U_i] \times (- \frac{1}{3}, \frac{1}{3}) \\ [(x,r)] \mapsto& (\varphi_i(x), r) \end{align*}

for the projection

\begin{align*} \pi: M \times \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \left(M \times \mathbb{R}\right)/\sim \end{align*}
