ideal under localization and empty intersection

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(S^{-1}R\) a localization and \(I \subseteq R\) an ideal and \(S^{-1}I\) the ideal under localization TFAE:

  1. \(I \cap S \neq \emptyset\)
  2. \(S^{-1}I = SR\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
