kernel of the evaluation homomorphism as maximal ideal for a field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(K[X_i \vert i \in I]\) the polynomial ring and \(\alpha_i \in K\) for \(i \in I\). Then the kernel \(\mathrm{ker}(f_{\alpha})\) of the evaluation homomorphism is a maximal ideal

\begin{align*} f_{\alpha}: K[X_i] \rightarrow K \\ \end{align*}

2. Proof

\(f_{\alpha}\) is surjective. Applying the Homomorphiesatz für Ringe results in

\begin{align*} K[X_i] / \mathrm{ker}(f_\alpha) \simeq K \end{align*}

since \(K\) is a field, we conclude, that \(\mathrm{ker}(f_\alpha)\) is maximal (see: maximales Ideal eines kommutativen Rings und Faktorring als Körper)
