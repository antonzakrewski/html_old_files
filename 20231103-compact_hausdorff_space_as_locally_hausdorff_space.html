compact hausdorff space as locally compact hausdorff space

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a compact Hausdorff space. Then \(X\) is locally compact Hausdorff

2. Proof

Let \(x \in X\) and \(O \subseteq X\) a neighbourhood of \(x\). Then \(X \setminus O\) and \(\{x\}\) are closed (cf. closed point and t1 space and Hausdorff-Raum impliziert Frechet Topologie)

Since \(X\) is a T4-Space (cf. compact hausdorff space as t4 space), there exist disjoint neighbourhood \(U_{X \setminus O}, U_{x}\). Thus \(X \setminus U_{X \setminus O}\) is closed, hence compact (cf. closed subset of a compact space as compact set) and a neighbourhood, as $Ux \subsetex X ∖ UX ∖ O
