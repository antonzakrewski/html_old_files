\begin{align*} \sum_{i=0}^{n} r_i X^i \cdot \sum_{i=0}^{n'} m_i X^i =& \sum_{k=0}^{n + n'} \sum_{i=0}^{k} r_i \cdot m_{i-k} \cdot X^k \end{align*}
