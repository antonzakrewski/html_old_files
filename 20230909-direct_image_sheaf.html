direct image sheaf

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T}_Y)\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{V}\) a category, \(\mathcal{F} \mathcal{G}\) V-valued sheaves Then for a topological space \((Y, \mathcal{T}_Y)\) and a continuous map \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) the direct image sheaf \(f_* \mathcal{F})\) is defined as sheaf on \(Y\) with

\begin{align*} (f_*\mathcal{F})(V) = \mathcal{F}(f^{-1}[V]) \end{align*}
