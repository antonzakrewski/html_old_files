exact sequence of modules and length

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M_1,M_2,M_3\) $R$-modules and

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 1 \arrow[]{r}{\varphi_1} & M_1 \arrow[]{r}{\varphi_2} & M_2 \arrow[]{r}{\varphi_3} M_3 \arrow[]{r}{} 1 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

an exact sequence. Then

\begin{align*} l(M_2) = l(M_1) + l(M_3) \end{align*}
