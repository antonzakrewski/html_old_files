finite polynomial ring over a field as noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(K[X_1,...,X_n]\) the polynomial ring in finite variables. Then \(K[X_1,...,X_n]\) is a noetherian ring

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Field as noetherian ring
  • Hilbert's basis theorem
