Krull's Hauptidealsatz

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian topological space and \(f \in A \setminus A^{\times}\) sucht that \(f\) is not a unit and not a zero divisor Then every minimal prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}\) with \(f \in \mathfrak{p}\) has height \(1\)
