image of a noetherian ring as noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring and \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) a homomorphism for a commutative ring \(B\). Then \(B\) is also noetherian.

2. Proof

Let \(I \subseteq B\) an ideal, then \(\varphi^{-1}[I]\) is also an ideal (see: preimage of an ideal as ideal). By assumption, \(I\) is finitely generated and hence \(\varphi[\varphi^{-1}[I]] = I\) is as an image of finitely generated ideal itself finitely generated
