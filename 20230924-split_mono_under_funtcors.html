split mono under funtcors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a covariant functor Suppose \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\) is a split mono, then \(\mathcal{F}(f)\) is also split mono
