projective object

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(P \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\). Then \(P\) is a projective object if it has the left lifting property against epimorphisms

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & M \arrow[twoheadrightarrow, ]{d}{\varphi} P \arrow[dashed]{ru}{\exists g} \arrow[]{r}{f} & N \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
