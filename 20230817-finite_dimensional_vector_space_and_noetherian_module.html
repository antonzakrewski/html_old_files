finite dimensional vector space and noetherian module

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(V\) a vector space. TFAE:

  1. \(V\) is finite dimensional
  2. \(V\) is a noetherian module

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
