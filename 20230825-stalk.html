stalk of a presheaf on a topological space

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a C-valued presheaf on a topological space \((X,\mathcal{T})\). Then the stalk \(\mathcal{F}_x\) at \(x \in X\) - if it exists - is defined as direct limit of the open neighbourhoods (cf. category of open Subsets as cofiltered category, sheaf as a contravariant functor)
