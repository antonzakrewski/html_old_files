homogeneous ideal as additive prime ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid, \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{h}\) an ideal

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{h}\) is a homogeneous ideal
  2. for \(r_{m_i} \in R_{m_i)\), \(m_i \neq m_j \Leftrightarrow i \neq j\), \(i \in \{1,...,n\}\) and \(\sum_{i=1}^{n} r_{m_i} \in \mathfrak{h}\) it follows that \(r_{m_i} \in \mathfrak{h}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

By Assumption, \(\mathfrak{h} = \bigoplus_{m \in M} (R_m \cap \mathfrak{h})\) and thus ∑i=1n rmi ∈ \bigoplusi =1n (Rmi ∩ \mathfrak{h})$ Thus \(r_{m_i} \in \mathfrak{h}\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
