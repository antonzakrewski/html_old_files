quotient ring of a noetherian ring as noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring and \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\) an ideal. Then \(A/\mathfrak{a}\) is also noetherian

2. Proof

corollary of image of a noetherian ring as noetherian ring for the canonical projection

\begin{align*} \pi_{\mathfrak{a}}: A \twoheadrightarrow A/\mathfrak{a} \end{align*}
