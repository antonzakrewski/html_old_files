Manifold uniquely defined by covering and transition maps

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a manifold and \(\mathcal{A}\) an atlas for \(M\). Then \(M\) is uniquely defined up to homeomorphisms by

  1. for the open covering \(O_i\) of \(M\) the open sets \(\varphi_i(O_i) \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n\) \(M\) from \(\mathcal{A}\)
  2. the transition maps

2. Proof

Let \(\mathrm{O}\) be the category containing the topological spaces \(O_i \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathrm{O})\) and the transition maps \(\varphi_{i,j}\).
