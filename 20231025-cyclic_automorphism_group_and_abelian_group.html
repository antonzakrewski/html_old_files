cyclic automorphism group and abelian group

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group and \(\mathrm{Aut}(G)\) the automorphism group. Suppose \(\mathrm{Aut}(G)\) is cyclic, then \(G\) is abelian

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Untergruppe einer zyklischen Gruppe als zyklische Gruppe
  • Group modulo center isomorphic to inner automorphism group
  • cyclic quotient group for the center and abelian group
