exact sequence and monomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(H,G\) be groups and \(0\) the zero group and \(\varphi: H \rightarrow G\) a homomorphism TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is a monomorphism
  2. the sequence \(1 \underrightarrow{\psi} H \underrightarrow{\varphi} G\) is exact at \(H\)

2. Proof

Using the characterization:

  • minimal kernel and monomorphism

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

It follows from \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) = \{e\} = \mathrm{im}(\psi)\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

since \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) = \mathrm{im}(\psi) = \{e\}\), we conclude, that the kernel is minimal and hence \(\varphi\) is injective
