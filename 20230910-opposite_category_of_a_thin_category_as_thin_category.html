opposite category of a thin category as thin category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a thin category, then the opposite category is also thin

2. Proof

follows from construction / contraposition if there exist \(f^{\mathrm{opp}} \neq g^{\mathrm{opp}}\) for \(f,g \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}}(A,B)\), then there also exist

\begin{align*} f,g \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(B,A) \\ f \neq g \end{align*}

Hence \(\mathcal{C}\) is not thin
