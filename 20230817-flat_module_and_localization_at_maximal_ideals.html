flat module and localization at maximal ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is a flat \(R\)-module
  2. \(M_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is a flat \(R_{\mathfrak{p}}\) module for any prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(R)\)
  3. \(M_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is a flat \(R_{\mathfrak{p}}\) module for any maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(R)\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

special case

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
