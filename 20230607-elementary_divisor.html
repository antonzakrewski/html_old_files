elementary divisor

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a principal ideal domain and \(M\) a finitely generated module. Then \(q_i \in R\) is an elementary divisor, if there exist \(q_i, i \in \{1,...,n\}\) such that

\begin{align*} M \simeq \bigoplus_{i=1}^n R /(q_i) \end{align*}

where \((q_i)\) are Primideal
