1. Definition

\begin{align*} (\alpha_1, ..., \alpha_n) (\beta_1,...,\beta_n) =& (\alpha_1 + \beta_1, ..., \alpha_n + \beta_n) \\ \alpha \cdot (\alpha_1, ..., \alpha_n) =& (\alpha \cdot \alpha_1, ..., \alpha \cdot \alpha_n) \end{align*}
