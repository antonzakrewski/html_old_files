Spectrum of a noetherian ring as noetherian space

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring, then the zariski spectrum \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) with the Zariski topology is a Noetherian topological space.

2. Proof

Suppose $V1 \supseteq V2 \supseteq V3 \supseteq …$ is a Chain of closed subsets. By assumption, each \(V_i\) is generated by an ideal \(\mathfrak{a}_i\). Furthermore, $\mathfrak{a}1 \subseteq \mathfrak{a}2 \subseteq … $ (see: Vanishing set and order reversing). Since \(A\) is noetherian, \(\mathfrak{a}_i\) stabilizes and therefore \(V(\mathfrak{a}_i)\) stabilizes.
