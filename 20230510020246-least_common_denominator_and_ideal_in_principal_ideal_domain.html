least common denominator and product of ideals in a principal ideal domain

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a principal ideal domain and \(a,b \in R\). Then for the least common denominator \(l \coloneqq \mathrm{lcd}(a,b)\) and the product of the ideals \(aR, bR\) following holds true:

\begin{align*} aR \cdot bR = \mathrm{lcd}(a,b) \end{align*}
