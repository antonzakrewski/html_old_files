integral element and finitely generated subring containing the element

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(B \subseteq A\) a subring and \(\alpha \in A\). TFAE:

  1. \(\alpha\) is integral over \(B\)
  2. there exists a finitely generated subring \(C \subseteq A\) such that \(B[\alpha] \subseteq C\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
