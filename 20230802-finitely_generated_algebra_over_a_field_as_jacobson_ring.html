finitely generated algebra over a field as jacobson ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(A\) a finitely generated K-Algebra. Then \(A\) is a Jacobson ring

2. Proof

let \(\mathfrak{p}\) be a prime ideal. Then

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p} \subseteq \bigcap_{\mathfrak{m} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(A) \vert \mathfrak{p} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}} \mathfrak{m} \\ \end{align*}

furthermore, the preimages of a maximal ideals for algebras over a field are maximal ideals, hence applying \(\pi: A \rightarrow A[X]\) we get

\begin{align*} \bigcap_{\mathfrak{m} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{max}}(A) \vert \mathfrak{p} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}} \mathfrak{m} \subseteq& \bigcap_{\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{rab}}(A) \vert \mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}} \\ \end{align*}

At last, applying radical of an ideal and intersection of rabinowitsch spectrum and prime ideal as radical ideal we get

\begin{align*} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}} \mathfrak{m} \subseteq& \bigcap_{\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}_{\mathrm{rab}}(A) \vert \mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}} =& \sqrt{\mathfrak{p}} \\ =& \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}
