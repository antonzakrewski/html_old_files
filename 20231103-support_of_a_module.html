support

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Then the support of \(M\), \(\mathrm{Supp}(M)\), is defined as set of prime ideals \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\), such that for the localization of a \(M\) at \(\mathfrak{p}\) it holds:

\begin{align*} M_\mathfrak{p} = 0 \end{align*}
