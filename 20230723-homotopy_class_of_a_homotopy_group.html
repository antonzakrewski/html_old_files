homotopy class of a homotopy group

1. Definition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space, then the homotopy classes of the n-th homotopy group consists of the homotopy classes

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}((I^n, \partial I^n),(X,x_0)) \end{align*}

modulo homotopy relative to \(\partial I^n\)
