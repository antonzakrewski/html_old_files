pullback as limit

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(P\) a pullback

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & P \arrow[]{rd}{\pi_1} \arrow[]{ld}{\pi_2}& \\ A \arrow[swap]{rd}{g} & & B \arrow[]{ld}{f}\\ & C & \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Then \(P\) is also a limit of the diagram

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[swap]{rd}{g}& & B \arrow[]{ld}{f} \\ & C & \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2. Proof

follows from definitions
