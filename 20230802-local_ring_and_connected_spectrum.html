local ring and connected spectrum

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a local ring. Then \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) is connected.

2. Proof

Let \(A_1,A_2 \subseteq \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) be disjoint closed subsets such that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}(A) = A_1 \sqcup A_2 \end{align*}

By assumption, w.l.o.g. \(\mathfrak{m} \not\in A_1\). Suppose \(\mathfrak{p} \in A_1\), then \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}\) and hence \(\mathfrak{m} \in A_1\). Therefore \(A_1\) is empty and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) connected
