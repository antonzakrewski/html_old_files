cokernel (category theory)

1. Definition

A cokernel is a kernel in the opposite category

2. Explicite

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category with a terminal object \(1\). Then the \(\mathrm{coker}(f)\) for a morphism \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\) - if it exists - is defined as pushout

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{r}{f} \arrow[]{d}{}& B \arrow[]{d}{\pi}\\ 1 \arrow[]{r}{} & \mathrm{coker}(f) \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
