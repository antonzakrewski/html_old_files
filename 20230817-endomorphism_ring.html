endomorphism ring

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Then the endomorphism ring \(\mathrm{End}(M)\) is defined as set of module-endomorphism with following operations

\begin{align*} (f + g)(x) \coloneqq f(x) + g(x) \\ (f \cdot g)(x) \coloneqq f \circ g(x) \end{align*}
