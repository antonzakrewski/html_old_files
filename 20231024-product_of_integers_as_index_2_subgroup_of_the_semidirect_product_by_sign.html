product of integers as index 2 subgroup of the semidirect product by sign

1. Proposition

the direct product \(\mathbb{Z} \rtimes \mathbb{Z}\) is isomorphic to a subgroup of \(\mathbb{Z} \rtimes \mathbb{Z}\) of the semidirect product with Index 2.

2. Proof

Let \(\mathbb{Z} \rimes 2 \mathbb{Z} \subseteq \mathbb{Z} \rtimes \mathbb{Z}\). Then we conclude that

\begin{align*} (a,2b) \cdot (a',2b') =& (a + (-1)^{2b} a', 2b + 2b') \\ =& (a + a', 2(b + b')) \end{align*}
