linear map as bilinear map for the product with the ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) \(R\)-modules and \(\varphi: M \rightarrow N\) an homomorphism. Then

\begin{align*} \varphi': R \times M \rightarrow N \\ (r,m) \mapsto r \cdot \varphi(m) \end{align*}

is an induced bilinear map
